### Featured Projects

**[Maya](https://github.com/OarisaR/Maya)** — Smart Maternal Health Assistant
A trimester-aware AI companion for pregnant women, combining RAG over 12,800+ chunks of WHO/CDC/NHS/ACOG clinical guidelines with a deterministic safety classifier that catches obstetric emergencies *before* any generative response — because the one thing you can't hallucinate is "this is safe."
`React` `FastAPI` `Pinecone` `Groq` · [Live Demo](https://maya.orebayet.workers.dev) · [Video](#)

**Senteroid** — Asteroid Impact & Exploration Simulator
An interactive planetary-defense platform that turns raw NASA NeoWS/JPL/USGS data into visualizations regular people can actually understand — simulate impacts, compare seismic energy to real earthquakes, and explore mitigation strategies.
`Live Site` · [Demo Video](#)

**MindScribe** — AI-Powered Mind Maps from Text
Turns dense lecture notes, papers, and textbook chapters into interactive concept maps you can explore, quiz yourself on, and track understanding through.
