## Featured Projects

**[Maya](https://github.com/OarisaR/Maya) — Smart Maternal Health Assistant**
A trimester-aware AI companion for pregnant women. Combines RAG over 12,800+ chunks of WHO, CDC, NHS, and ACOG clinical guidelines with a deterministic safety classifier that flags obstetric emergencies before any generative response is produced.
`React` `FastAPI` `Pinecone` `Groq`
[Live Demo](https://maya.orebayet.workers.dev) · Video (coming soon)

**[Senteroid](https://github.com/KrishnenduBarua/Senteroid) — Asteroid Impact & Exploration Simulator**
An interactive planetary-defense platform that turns raw NASA NeoWS, JPL, and USGS data into visualizations for a general audience — simulate impacts, compare seismic energy to real earthquakes, and explore mitigation strategies.
[Live Site](https://senteroid.vercel.app/) · [Watch Demo](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6YesOB8vKQA)

**[MindScribe](https://github.com/OarisaR/mind-scribe) — AI-Powered Mind Maps from Text**
Converts dense lecture notes, papers, and textbook chapters into interactive concept maps that support exploration, self-quizzing, and progress tracking.
[Live Site](https://mindscribe-2026o.web.app/) · [Watch Demo](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dXaIf_BVEA4)
